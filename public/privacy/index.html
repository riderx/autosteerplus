autosteerplus privacy policy

This page explains, in simple terms, what autosteerplus handles when you use the hosted website or the mobile app.

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Back to portal GitHub repository

What autosteerplus is

autosteerplus is a hosted web fork and a mobile app wrapper around the published portal at fsd.teslaandroid.com. The hosted site and app talk to the live portal backend for account, package, and session data.

What data is used

  • Account sign-in data such as your email address and password, when you sign in.
  • Session cookies and CSRF tokens needed to keep your signed-in session working.
  • Device context such as connected device name, firmware version, and OTA capability, when you connect hardware in the portal.
  • Bluetooth traffic needed for live device management in the app or in supported Web Bluetooth browsers.

What the site does not try to collect

  • No extra advertising profile is built by autosteerplus from your portal usage.
  • No separate analytics layer is intentionally added here beyond the infrastructure already involved in serving the site.
  • Bluetooth permissions are only used for direct device management features.

Cookies and sessions

The hosted site uses secure session cookies so your login can stay active across page reloads. Those cookies are required for sign-in, account access, and protected portal actions.

Bluetooth and device communication

When you connect a device, the portal exchanges data needed to read status, update configuration, and perform OTA operations. On iPhone and Android, the native app is the preferred path because it bridges Bluetooth directly through Capacitor.

Third parties involved

  • Cloudflare serves the hosted website and Workers infrastructure.
  • The live portal backend remains the source for session and account behavior.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play / Bluefy may be relevant depending on how you access the portal.

Your choice

If you do not want to use the hosted web fork, you can stop using the site. If you want to end an active session, log out from the portal.

Contact

For project-level questions, use the links already provided in the portal, GitHub repository, or Slack community.
